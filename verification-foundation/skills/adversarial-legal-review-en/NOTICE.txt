ADVERSARIAL LEGAL REVIEW (adversarial-legal-review-en) - NOTICE

A red-team / devil's-advocate review for a high-stakes deliverable.
The debate + 3-layer verification pattern is inspired by AnttiHero/lavern (Apache 2.0);
bounded self-revision, reviewer access tiers and the always-deliver invariant are adapted
clean-room from gregmos/memoforge (MIT) - concepts only, not prompts or code. Roles,
prompts and the 10-point check are written from scratch. Packaged for MateMatic Boutique
by MateMatic Solutions Sp. z o.o. Polish counterpart: adversarial-legal-review-pl.
